Targeting fundamental reservoir metrics for long-term production optimization and sustained value creation.

Axerra's chemistry moves beyond simple stimulation to provide engineered solutions that operate at the level of fluid mechanics, rock physics, and production economics — delivering measurable, verifiable improvements to the KPIs that define a well's profitability.

Operational Value Drivers

Focused on Performance Metrics. One Engineered System.

01 monitoring
KPI: Barrels Per Day

Daily Production Rates

By fundamentally altering fluid rheology within the reservoir, Axerra drives a step-change in deliverability. According to Darcy's Law, flow rate is inversely proportional to viscosity — reducing viscosity directly translates to higher throughput.

Laboratory performance: Viscosity reduced from over 100,000 cSt to less than 38 cSt against representative crude samples.
Field result: Production increases of 100% to over 500% documented within the first 14 days post-treatment.

02 water_drop
KPI: Water Cut %

Water Cut & Sweep Efficiency

By optimizing the Mobility Ratio, Axerra ensures pumps are lifting oil rather than water. Lowering oil viscosity brings the mobility of the oil phase closer to that of the water phase — stabilizing flood fronts and preventing water fingering.

Mechanism: Viscosity reduction stabilizes the displacement front, preventing water from bypassing trapped oil in the reservoir matrix.
Field result: Water cuts reduced by up to 30% in heavy oil wells, significantly improving fractional oil flow.
water_drop
03 price_check
KPI: $/bbl Lifting Cost

Lifting Costs & OPEX Reduction

Viscosity reduction decreases the mechanical resistance of the fluid column, producing direct and measurable savings in energy consumption, surface equipment load, and downhole maintenance cycles.

Friction reduction: Thinner oil lowers drag against tubing walls and flowlines, reducing overall line pressure requirements.
Energy efficiency: Reduced fluid resistance allows surface motors to operate at lower amperage — directly reducing monthly utility costs.
Equipment protection: Chemistry overcomes dry pump and fluid pound conditions, protecting downhole equipment from mechanical fatigue.

04 trending_down
KPI: Production Decline Rate

Decline Curve Management

Axerra flattens the production decline curve by unlocking hydrocarbons in tight rock and micro-fractures that traditional completions cannot reach — specifically targeting wells where production falls faster than expected due to permeability impairment.

Penetration mechanism: Sub-nanometer particles leverage Brownian motion and disjoining pressure to access fracture networks where proppants cannot reach.
Long-term efficacy: Treatment provides durable hydrocarbon recovery improvements that persist beyond the initial stimulation window.

Flow Assurance & MTBF

Axerra improves Flow Assurance by preventing the organic and inorganic deposition events that cause unplanned downtime and costly rig interventions — addressing the problem at the chemistry level rather than through reactive remediation.

Solids suspension: Surfactant package acts as a dispersant, preventing paraffins and asphaltenes from plating out on tubing ID.
Operational result: Reduced rod breaks and tubing cleans extend asset operational life and reduce high-cost workover event frequency.

07
KPI: MTBF & Lifting Cost/bbl

Asset Longevity & Mechanical Integrity

Fundamental viscosity reduction eliminates the high-stress operating conditions — dry pump and fluid pound — that compromise artificial lift integrity and drive premature equipment failure in heavy oil assets. Moving high-viscosity, non-Newtonian fluids through narrow tubing creates extreme friction and sluggish intake speeds. The pump often outpaces the fluid's ability to fill the barrel, leading to mechanical vibration, rod float, and catastrophic rod breaks.

By thinning the oil column from over 100,000 cSt to under 50 cSt, Axerra's technology ensures the pump remains constantly primed — reducing reciprocating torque on the surface unit and minimizing peak polished rod load.
