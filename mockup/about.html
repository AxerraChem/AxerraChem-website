About Axerra

Engineered for the Harshest Environments.

Axerra Chemical is a specialty chemical engineering company focused entirely on enhanced oil recovery. We exist to maximize the productive life of the world's existing hydrocarbon assets.

Our Mission

Unlocking What Is Already There.

The world produces approximately 30 billion barrels of oil per year, yet leaves 60–70% of reserves in the ground. Axerra was founded on one conviction: that precision chemistry can close that gap — field by field, reservoir by reservoir.

We don't sell commodity chemicals. We engineer bespoke molecular solutions for specific subsurface environments, and we remain committed partners through every phase of deployment — from the laboratory bench to full-field production.
